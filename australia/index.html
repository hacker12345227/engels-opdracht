📖 Travel Diary

When I arrived in Australia, everything felt huge and open. The sky seemed endless and the air felt dry and warm.

I visited Sydney first. Seeing the Opera House in real life was incredible, especially near the harbour at sunset.

Later I traveled into the Outback. The landscape became red, empty, and quiet. It felt like I was on another planet.

One of the most amazing moments was seeing kangaroos in the wild. It felt unreal to be so close to nature like that.

🏙️ Tourist Attractions

  • Sydney Opera House
  • Great Barrier Reef
  • Uluru (Ayers Rock)
  • Great Ocean Road

📜 Historical Facts

  • Aboriginal people are the original inhabitants
  • British colonization started in 1788
  • Australia became independent in 1901
  • One of the most multicultural countries

🎌 Holidays

  • Australia Day
  • Anzac Day

🏜️ Landscape

Australia has deserts, rainforests, beaches, coral reefs and huge open landscapes.

🎎 Culture

  • Relaxed and friendly lifestyle
  • Strong sports culture
  • Barbecue (“BBQ”) traditions
  • Influences from many cultures
