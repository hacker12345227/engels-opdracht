📖 Travel Diary

South Africa felt adventurous from the moment I arrived. The mix of wildlife, mountains and cities made every day exciting.

I started my journey in Cape Town, where I visited Table Mountain. The view over the ocean and the city was incredible.

Later, I went on a safari in Kruger National Park. Seeing lions, elephants and giraffes in the wild was unforgettable.

One evening, I watched the sunset in the savannah while hearing animals in the distance. It felt like a scene from a movie.

📜 Historical Facts

🌍 Landscape

South Africa has savannahs, mountains, beaches, deserts and wildlife reserves.

🎎 Culture
