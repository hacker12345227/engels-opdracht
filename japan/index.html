📖 Travel Diary

When I arrived in Japan, I immediately felt like I was in a different world. The bright lights of Tokyo surrounded me, and everything moved so fast.

One evening, I walked through Shibuya Crossing. Thousands of people crossed the street at the same time. It felt like I was part of something huge.

A few days later, I visited Kyoto. Everything became quiet and peaceful. I walked through the red gates of Fushimi Inari Shrine, and it felt almost magical.

My favorite moment was seeing Mount Fuji in the distance. It looked perfect, like a painting. I will never forget that view.

🏯 Tourist Attractions

  • Mount Fuji
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Shibuya Crossing
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Forest

📜 Historical Facts

  • Samurai played an important role in history
  • The Edo period brought long peace
  • Japan modernized after 1868
  • Atomic bombs were dropped in 1945

🎌 Holidays

  • Hanami (Cherry Blossom Festival)
  • Obon Festival

🌋 Landscape

Japan has mountains, volcanoes, forests, and large modern cities.

🎎 Culture

  • Respect and politeness are very important
  • Traditional tea ceremonies
  • Anime and manga culture
  • Mix of tradition and technology
